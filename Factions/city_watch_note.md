# City Watch

The official law enforcement organization responsible for maintaining order within the city. Struggles with corruption and limited resources while trying to combat organized crime.

## Goals
- Maintain law and order
- Combat organized crime
- Root out internal corruption

## Resources
- Official authority
- Patrol networks
- Detention facilities
- Informant network

## Key NPCs
- Captain James Ironside
- Sergeant Sarah Nightwatch
- Investigator Thomas Truthseeker

## Operations
The City Watch maintains regular patrols throughout the city districts, with increased presence in merchant quarters and dock areas. They operate from the central Watch House and coordinate with the local militia for major operations. Despite their efforts, corruption within their ranks provides opportunities for criminal organizations to operate.
