# Smuggler's Consortium

An underground network of smugglers, black market dealers, and corrupt officials. They operate in the shadows, moving contraband and avoiding official trade taxes.

## Goals
- Undermine official trade monopolies
- Expand black market operations
- Avoid law enforcement

## Resources
- Hidden routes and safe houses
- Bribed officials
- Fast ships and skilled crews
- Underground contacts

## Key NPCs
- Captain "Blackwater" Ben
- Fence Master Tilly Quickfingers
- Corrupt Harbormaster Jim Greased-Palm

## Operations
The Consortium operates through a network of safe houses and hidden caches throughout the city's underground. They move goods through secret tunnels and use coded signals to coordinate with dock workers who help load and unload contraband under cover of legitimate cargo.
