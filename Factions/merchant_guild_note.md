# The Merchant Guild

A powerful trade organization controlling most commercial activity in the region. Known for their extensive network of contacts and significant political influence through economic leverage.

## Goals
- Maintain trade route security
- Expand market influence
- Eliminate smuggling competition

## Resources
- Large treasury reserves
- Merchant fleet
- Political connections
- Information network

## Key NPCs
- Guildmaster Helena Goldweaver
- Trade Captain Marcus Ironhand
- Spymaster "Silent" Sarah

## Operations
The Merchant Guild operates legitimate trade routes throughout the region, maintaining warehouses in major cities and establishing trade agreements with foreign kingdoms. They work closely with the Harbor Authority to ensure smooth operations and maintain their own security forces to protect valuable cargo shipments.
